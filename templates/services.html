  • Research

    • Research Fields and Directions

    Li Y, Yuan H, von dem Bussche A, et al. Graphene microsheets enter cells through spontaneous membrane penetration at edge asperities and corner sites[J]. Proceedings of the National Academy of Sciences, 2013, 110(30): 12295-12300.

    • Biomechanics

      The penetration and interaction between graphene and cell membrane have been investigated, and the mechanism has been revealed systematically. It can contribute to the drug transport and other medical applications.

    • Nanomaterials

      Mechanical and thermal properties of nanomaterials have been investigated. Some fascinating results have been revealed and interpreted.

    Li Y, Datta D, Li Z, et al. Mechanical properties of hydrogen functionalized graphene allotropes[J]. Computational Materials Science, 2014, 83: 212-216.

    1. Li Y, Wei A, Datta D. Thermal characteristics of graphene nanoribbons endorsed by surface functionalization[J]. Carbon, 2017, 113: 274-282.

    2. Li Y, Datta D, Li Z. Anomalous mechanical characteristics of graphene with tilt grain boundaries tuned by hydrogenation[J]. Carbon, 2015, 90: 234-241.

    • Surface Modification

      Using hydrogenation, doping and hybrization etc. methods, we try to make modifications on the surface of 2D nanomaterial, in order to control some properties in applications.

    • Molecular dynamics

      Using molecular dynamics simulation method, we can calculate the tensile strength, stress distribution, thermal conductivity etc. and simulate the movement of atoms. It's an efficient and important way to investigate the properties of nanomaterials.

    2. Li Y, Datta D, Li Z. Anomalous mechanical characteristics of graphene with tilt grain boundaries tuned by hydrogenation[J]. Carbon, 2015, 90: 234-241.
